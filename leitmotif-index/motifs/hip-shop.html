JCoxeye's Leitmotif Index:
'Hip Shop'

Summary


('Hip Shop', as heard in Hip Shop)

'Hip Shop' is a very minor motif mainly used in relation to certain shops, those being Rouxls Kaard's Hip Shop and Swatch's, uh, other Hip Shop.

The motif also appears in Green Room, the theme of the Green Room as shown in the Deltarune September 2022 Status Update.
