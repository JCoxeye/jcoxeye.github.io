JCoxeye's Leitmotif Index:
'Jingle Bells'

Summary


('Jingle Bells', as heard in Holiday Studio)

It is Jingle Bells.

Appearances

Ghouliday

'Jingle Bells': 0:00-0:11

Note that in Ghouliday, 'Jingle Bells' is mainly present in rhythm, with a modified melody.

Also, when playing the song in-game, Napstablook sings along.

Holiday Studio

'Jingle Bells': 0:00-0:33

(See also: 'Sweet Cap'n Cakes')

Additionally, in the Spamton Sweepstakes, Noelle's blog has two pages with Jingle Bells as their music:

  • /code/, with 'Blood Crushers The Band - Raise Up Your Bat'
  • /catpetterz/, with 'Cat Petterz 2 OST (Corrupted) - No Cats'

'Blood Crushers The Band - Raise Up Your Bat' is actually a modified version of a MIDI file seemingly downloaded from westnet.com; ("gs_jbl2.mid: Jingle Bells (Country Feel)" under 'Others').

Notably, the percussion is different, but the arrangement is definitely the same - it's possible Toby actually got the file from somewhere else, or modified it himself.

Meanwhile 'Cat Petterz 2 OST (Corrupted) - No Cats' appears to be a 'corrupted' version of Jingle Bells (Country Feel), but without painstakingly un-corrupting the entire song, I can't be 100% sure of that.
