JCoxeye's Leitmotif Index:
'Power of NEO'

Summary


('Power of NEO', as heard in Battle Against a True Hero)

The 'Power of NEO' motif first appears in Battle Against a True Hero, which is then referenced in Power of "NEO", which is itself referenced in Deltarune's BIG SHOT.

Appearances

Battle Against a True Hero

'Power of NEO': 0:00-1:35

(See also: 'Ruins')

Power of "NEO"

'Power of NEO': 0:00-0:26

BIG SHOT

In BIG SHOT's middle section, 'Power of NEO' is played alongside 'THE WORLD REVOLVING':

'Power of NEO': 0:54-1:22

(In the orchestral hits in the back, panned right)
(Backed up by the chiptune synth in the back, panned left)

Later in the song, Power of "NEO" 's instruments are reused for the 'Spamton, Part 2', and a quote of 'Power of NEO' is slipped in:

'Power of NEO': 2:08-2:10
