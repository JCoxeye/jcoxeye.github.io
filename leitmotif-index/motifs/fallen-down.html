JCoxeye's Leitmotif Index:
'Fallen Down'

Summary


('Fallen Down A' and 'Fallen Down B', as heard in Fallen Down)

'Fallen Down' is the theme of Toriel.
It only appears in a few songs, all with direct relation to her.

Notably, Fallen Down was orignally composed for the 2012 Earthbound tribute album I Miss You , as discussed here:
On the Origins of Undertale and Deltarune's Leitmotifs

Appearances

Fallen Down

'Fallen Down A': 0:00-0:42

'Fallen Down B': 0:13-0:42

(Note that the OST version of the song is slightly slower, and plays an extra phrase of 'Fallen Down A' before progressing to 'Fallen Down B')

The slowed-down version of Fallen Down that plays in Toriel's house after killing her, Empty House, (So called by the Undertale Demo OST ) is identical in every way other than speed, and so it won't be discussed here.

Fallen Down (Reprise)

'Fallen Down A': 0:00-0:52

'Fallen Down B': 0:25-0:52

From there, the song remixes 'Fallen Down' into a somewhat unique melody:

'Fallen Down': 0:52-1:18

Afterwards, it transitions into 'Once Upon a Time', syncopated into 3/4 time.
