'Rouxls Kaard' is the theme of Rouxls Kaard (pronounced "Rules Card"), and is accordingly pretentious and over-the-top:


('Rouxls Kaard', as heard in Rouxls Kaard)

Worth noting is that it also has a backing melody:


('Rouxls Kaard (Backing)', as heard in Epic Rouxls Battle)
