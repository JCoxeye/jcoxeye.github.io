JCoxeye's Leitmotif Index:
'Spamton'

Summary

'Spamton' is (obviously) the motif of Spamton, and is split into two relatively distinct parts:

A standard theme, heard in most of his songs:

('Spamton, Part 1', as heard in Spamton)

And a battle theme, heard mainly is his fights:

('Spamton, Part 2', as heard in NOW'S YOUR CHANCE TO BE A )

Finally, Spamton also has an extraneous melody, heard in a couple of songs:


('HEY EVERY !', as heard in A Real Boy! )

('HEY EVERY !'s appearances will be covered first, because they're kept separate from Spamton's other themes)

Worth noting is that, just as Spamton's name and ultimate form (Spamton NEO) reference Mettaton, so too do his motifs:
'Spamton, Part 1' is akin to Mettaton's standard theme, 'It's Showtime!',
'Spamton, Part 2' is akin to Mettaton's battle theme, 'Metal Crusher',
and 'HEY EVERY !' is akin to Mettaton's extraneous theme, 'Oh!'.

Additionally, as BIG SHOT is the only other song to quote them, this article also covers the 'Dummy!' leitmotif, both its coda and its bridge:


('Dummy! (Bridge)', as heard in Dummy! )


('Dummy! (Coda)', as heard in Dummy! )

Beatdown (Strider Style)

Finally, let's end off with a bang.
The 'bang' being Homestuck, of course.

In Beatdown (Strider Style), the bassline of the song bears a striking resemblance to 'Spamton, Part 2':

'Spamton, Part 2': 0:07-1:15, 1:22-2:30

(Note that this bassline is occasionally incorporated into the song's main melody later in the song, and remixed)

'Spamton, Part 2', for reference:

The rhythms are nearly identical, and aside from how 'Spamton, Part 2' starts ascending one note earlier, the melodies are, for the most part, very similar.

There is an issue, though.

Two, actually:

  1. 'Spamton, Part 2's melody progresses past Beatdown (Strider Style) 's phrase, with variations of the melody making up the full motif
  2. Unlike other non-UT/DR tracks the games reference, Beatdown (Strider Style) is not a Toby Fox composition: it was composed by Curt Blakeslee, A.K.A. NOROCK

Ultimately, I'm not sure I can come to a conclusion on this; make of it what you will.
