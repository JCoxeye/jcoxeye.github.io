JCoxeye's Leitmotif Index:
'It's Showtime!'

Summary


('It's Showtime!', as heard in It's Showtime!)

'It's Showtime!', alongside 'Metal Crusher', 'Hotel', and 'Oh!', serve as Mettaton's leitmotifs, appearing in various contexts relating to the character.
(Mind you, it's highly recommended to read these articles together!)

'It's Showtime!' serves as Mettaton's main motif, appearing in most of his themes.
