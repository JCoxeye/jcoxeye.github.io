JCoxeye's Leitmotif Index:
'Uwa!!'

Summary


('Uwa!!', as heard in Uwa!! So Temperate♫)

'Uwa!!' is the theme of Undertale's controller config menu.
Depending on the system clock, the audio and visuals of the screen change in accordance with the seasons:

  • Uwa!! So Temperate♫ for Fall (September, October, and November)
  • Uwa!! So Holiday♫ for Winter (December, January, and February)
  • Uwa!! So Temperate♫ (again) for Spring (March, April, and May)
  • Uwa!! So HEATS!!♫ for Summer (June, July, and August)

Curiously, the motif also appears in the music for Deltarune.com's 404 page of all things, upon clicking on the sleeping dog.
(Do note that there is a small chance that the sleeping dog is replaced by a dog with maracas, which does not play results.ogg)
