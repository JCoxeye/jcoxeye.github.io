JCoxeye's Leitmotif Index:
'Alphys'

Summary


('Alphys' parts A, B, and C, as heard in Alphys)

'Alphys' is the theme of Alphys, as heard in Here We Are, and, y'know, Alphys.

It also appears in two other songs:

    She's Playing Piano
    (the 'She' referring to Undyne, reflecting their relationship)

    Bird That Carries You Over a Disproportionately Small Gap
    (reflecting the similar nature of the characters in question: underconfident and yellow)

Appearances

Bird That Carries You Over a Disproportionately Small Gap

'Alphys A': 0:06-0:18

Alphys

'Alphys A': 0:08-0:29

Leading up to its proper appearance later in the song, 'Alphys B' is tentatively played in the bassline at several points beforehand:

'Alphys B': 0:05-0:08, 0:13-0:15, 0:20-0:22, 0:28-0:31

'Alphys B': 0:31-1:00

Finally, the song is capped off with 'Alphys C:

'Alphys C': 1:00-1:24

She's Playing Piano

'Alphys C': 0:00-0:08

Here We Are

In Here We Are, the motif is syncopated into a combined 5/4 (or 10/8 if you prefer) time signature, consisting of two 3/4 measures and one 4/4 measure at the end:

'Alphys A': 0:00-0:44

'Alphys AB': 0:44-1:28

('Alphys B' being in the second half of the faster piano)

'Alphys C': 1:28-1:50

(In the ghostly voice in the background, as heard for 'Alphys A')
