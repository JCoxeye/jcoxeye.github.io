JCoxeye's Leitmotif Index:
'Alphys'

Summary


('Alphys' parts 1 through 3, as heard in Alphys)

'Alphys' is the theme of Alphys, as heard in Here We Are, and, y'know, Alphys.

It also appears in two other songs:

    She's Playing Piano
    (the 'She' referring to Undyne, reflecting their relationship)

    Bird That Carries You Over a Disproportionately Small Gap
    (reflecting the similar nature of the characters in question: underconfident and yellow)

Appearances

Bird That Carries You Over a Disproportionately Small Gap

'Alphys, Part 1': 0:06-0:18

Alphys

'Alphys, Part 1': 0:08-0:29

Leading up to its proper appearance later in the song, 'Alphys, Part 2' is tentatively played in the bassline at several points beforehand:

'Alphys, Part 2': 0:05-0:08, 0:13-0:15, 0:20-0:22, 0:28-0:31

'Alphys, Part 2': 0:31-1:00

Finally, the song is capped off with 'Alphys, Part 3':

'Alphys, Part 3': 1:00-1:24

She's Playing Piano

'Alphys, Part 3': 0:00-0:08

Here We Are

In Here We Are, the motif is syncopated into a combined 5/4 (or 10/8 if you prefer) time signature, consisting of two 3/4 measures and one 4/4 measure at the end:

'Alphys, Part 1': 0:00-0:44

'Alphys, Parts 1 and 2': 0:44-1:28

('Alphys, Part 2' being in the second half of the faster piano)

'Alphys, Part 3': 1:28-1:50

(In the ghostly voice in the background, as heard for part 1)
