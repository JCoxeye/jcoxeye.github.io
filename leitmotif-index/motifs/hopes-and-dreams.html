JCoxeye's Leitmotif Index:
'Hopes and Dreams'

Summary


('Hopes and Dreams', as heard in Snowdin Town)

'Hopes and Dreams' first appears in Snowdin, in Snowdin Town, Shop, and the dating themes, serving a supporting role to the area's main theme, 'Snowy'.

Unlike 'Snowy' however, 'Hopes and Dreams' continues to appear throughout the game, culminating in the True Pacifist route's final boss theme, Hopes and Dreams.

In Deltarune, the motif reappears alongside the 'Hometown' leitmotif.
