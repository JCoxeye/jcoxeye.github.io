JCoxeye's Leitmotif Index:
'Another Medium'

Summary


('Another Medium' as heard in Waterfall)

'Another Medium' is most notably used in the overworld themes of Waterfall, Hotland, and the CORE, culminating in Mettaton EX's battle theme, Death By Glamour (and by extension, Bring It In, Guys! ).

The motif is also debatably heard in An Ending, Don't Give Up, Respite, and (even more debatably) Reunited. A proper analysis of those is forthcoming.

In CORE, Death By Glamour, and Bring It In, Guys!, 'Another Medium' is accompanied by the CORE's own leitmotif, 'CORE':


('CORE' as heard in CORE )

This article will also cover the 'Hotland' motif, which appears overtop of 'Another Medium' in Another Medium, CORE, and Bring It In, Guys! :


('Hotland' parts 1 and 2, as heard in Another Medium)

Finally, it should be noted that the 'Another Medium' leitmotif originates from a prior Toby Fox composition, Patient, as discussed at the bottom of the page.

Patient

Patient, composed by Toby Fox, is a remix of Doctor, the background music for the Land of Wind and Shade from Homestuck.

There are a few things of note about this:

  1. The Land of Wind and Shade strongly resembles Waterfall, with its blue, rocky design.
  2. The Land of Wind and Shade is a planet in the Medium, from which Another Medium evidently dervies its name.
  3. Patient Appears to be the origin of the 'Another Medium' leitmotif itself:

'Another Medium': 0:29-0:59

This isn't the only time Tob Fox has re-used a melody from previous projects for use in Undertale and Deltarune - just as 'Another Medium' comes from Patient,
'Metal Crusher' comes from Noisemaster,
'The Door' comes from Nightmare Knight,
and 'THE HOLY' comes from Penumbra Phantasm.
