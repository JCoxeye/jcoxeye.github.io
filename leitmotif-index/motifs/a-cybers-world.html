JCoxeye's Leitmotif Index:
'A CYBER'S WORLD'

Summary

'A CYBER'S WORLD' is the leitmotif of the "CYBER'S WORLD" [sic], which appears in A CYBER'S WORLD? and WELCOME TO THE CITY, as well as WELCOME TO THE CITY 's alternate and unused variations.

The motif itself is split into two relatively distinct parts:


('A CYBER'S WORLD' parts 1 and 2, as heard in WELCOME TO THE CITY )

Additionally, 'Sweet Cap'n Cakes, Part 1' is used as a backing melody in all appearances of 'A CYBER'S WORLD'.

'A CYBER'S WORLD, PART 2': 1:05-1:38
