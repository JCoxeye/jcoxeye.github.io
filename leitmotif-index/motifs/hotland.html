JCoxeye's Leitmotif Index:
'Hotland'

Summary


('Hotland' parts 1 and 2, as heard in Another Medium)

'Hotland' is of course the theme of Hotland, appearing in both Another Medium and CORE, as well as the Hotland section of Bring It In, Guys!.

'Hotland' appears exclusively alongside 'Another Medium', though the latter often appears without the former, such as in Waterfall.
