JCoxeye's Leitmotif Index:
'Memory'

Summary


('Memory', as heard in Memory)

Although it only appears in five songs, 'Memory' is nonetheless a very important leitmotif in Undertale 's soundtrack, representing the story's (arguably) most important character, Asriel.

Appearances

Memory

'Memory': 0:00-1:11

Interestingly, 'Memory' has an extra note in this track, as opposed to its other appearances.
It doesn't have an effect on the melody's overall sound, but it was enough for the piano puzzle partly through Waterfall to accept both variations of the motif.
