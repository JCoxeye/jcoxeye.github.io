JCoxeye's Leitmotif Index:
'Metal Crusher'

Summary


('Metal Crusher', as heard in Metal Crusher)

'Metal Crusher', alongside 'It's Showtime!', 'Hotel', and 'Oh!', serve as Mettaton's leitmotifs, appearing in various contexts relating to the character.
(Mind you, it's highly recommended to read these articles together!)

'Metal Crusher' serves as Mettaton's battle motif, appearing in both Metal Crusher and Death By Glamour (with a cameo appearance in Last Episode!).

Finally, Metal Crusher's beginning jingle (which in and of itself sounds very similar to the 'Metal Crusher' motif) is heard on its own elsewhere in the soundtrack, and will be covered here:

Noisemaster

Noisemaster is a (non-UTDR) fan song composed by Toby Fox for the character Noisemaster from Cucumber Quest.

Cucumber Quest, written by Gigi D.G., is a web comic 'about bunny kids going on adventures', and is (as of writing) on indefinite hiatus.
Gigi D.G. is also credited for various pieces of concept art for Deltarune.

Importantly, Noisemaster appears to be the origin of the 'Metal Crusher' motif itself:

'Metal Crusher': 0:35-0:52, 1:28-1:44

If this all sounds familiar, it's because it's almost the exact same story as Nightmare Knight, and the motif derived from it, 'The Door'.
It's also a similar story to Patient with 'Another Medium', and Penumbra Phantasm with 'THE HOLY'.
