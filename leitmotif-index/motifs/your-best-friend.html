JCoxeye's Leitmotif Index:
'Your Best Friend'

Summary


('Your Best Friend', as heard in Your Best Friend)

'Your Best Friend' is the leitmotif of Flowey.

Notably, it appears in Deltarune exactly once: in the honks heard during the traffic jam at the beginning of Chapter 2.
This is distinctly opposed to 'Your Best Nightmare', which functions as Flowey's secondary motif in Undertale, but is given new life in multiple Deltarune tracks.

Appearances

Your Best Friend

'Your Best Friend': 0:07-0:22

mus_toomuch.ogg

mus_toomuch.ogg is the music that plays upon encountering the 'But nobody came.' message after exhausting an area's kill count (most prominently seen during the Genocide/No Mercy route).

Speeding it up to 13.333x speed reveals it's the first quarter of the 'Your Best Friend' motif.

(Be warned, it's a little tough on the ears)

Your Best Nightmare

In the files, Your Best Nightmare is split into numerous different sound files, which in the soundtrack manifests as various distinct 'sections' of the song, namely six SOUL Phases, alongside some transitory phases.

First, the six SOUL Phases:

'Your Best Friend': 0:59-1:09

'Your Best Friend': 1:15-1:25

'Your Best Friend': 2:01-2:11

'Your Best Friend': 2:17-2:27

'Your Best Friend': 3:00-3:10

'Your Best Friend': 3:16-3:26

And finally, the transitory phase between the Orange SOUL Phase and Blue SOUL Phase, which would normally contain 'Your Best Nightmare' as indicated by the structure of the song, instead contains a heavily distorted version of the 'Your Best Friend' motif:

'Your Best Friend': 1:26-1:47

Finale

'Your Best Friend': 0:00-0:40, 0:57-1:52

(See also: 'Memory')

mus_f_finale_1 is an unused, shorter version of mus_f_finale_1_l, the first sound file that makes up Finale in-game (and the one shown above).
It's not really any different, and so it won't be covered here.

'Your Best Friend': 1:29-1:52

(See also: 'Hopes and Dreams')

SAVE the World

In the opening of SAVE the World, 'Your Best Friend' is interspliced with 'Once Upon a Time, Part 1' and 'Home':

'Your Best Friend' on its own:

Later, 'Your Best Friend' plays underneath 'Once Upon a Time, Part 3' like in Hopes and Dreams, but never breaks through to the foreground:
