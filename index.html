This is me, JCoxeye! ->

(Kris PFP courtesy of Discord user King Blocky)

Online, I'm pretty much exclusively active in relation to Undertale and Deltarune (particularly in the ACDS Discord Server, and have made a hobby of amateur web design, software engineering, and musical analysis.

More importantly than a rough, stilted overview of myself, I'm more than happy to discuss/give advice on what I've mentioned here.

In particular, I already have guides to Undertale's No Mercy/Genocide route and its bosses, and am working on a big essay/thesis/misguided trudge through the leitmotifs of it and Deltarune.

Feel free to contact me at pretty much any time!
(Discord: @jcoxeye)

What programs did I use to make this?

For hosting, I initially used Neocities, but switched to another hosting service due to being unable to upload audio and video content as freely as I would like (rather a big problem for the aforementioned leitmotif index). (?) For the record, the only reason I left was because I got a domain for free from a friend, so don't let this dissuade you from checking it out.

Neocities is an outstanding resource, and I highly recommend it if you're at all interested in web design!

For HTML/CSS/JS editing, I use Notepad++ on the PC, and Textastic on iPad (with an attached keyboard, obviously).

I also use Undertale Mod Tool for UT/DR code-diving and Audacium for isolating clips of leitmotifs.
